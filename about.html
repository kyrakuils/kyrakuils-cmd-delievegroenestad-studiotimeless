A B O U T

In 1997 they started Atlantis Trading, specializing in Larimar jewelry from the Dominican Republic. What began as a store with specific gemstones evolved into something popular, attending gemstone fairs worldwide. In 2007, they opened their first store called: Caribbean Creation in Amsterdam, showcasing diverse collections. During their travels, they discovered unique second- hand designer bags. They decided to add those bags to their product range. They now sell 100% authentic and antique handbags with charming stories added to their shop.

They realized their small shop on Gravenstraat in Amsterdam didn't showcase the special bags well, so they moved and opened a new store in Amsterdam just for their vintage luxury collection. The store opened in July 2013 at the Oude Spiegelstraat 1. Questions? Feel free to contact us.
